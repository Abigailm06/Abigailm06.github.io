How To Make: Spicy Shrimp Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Shrimp of your choice (small, regular, jumbo - tails on or removed)
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Grated Parmsean Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta based on the amount of time the packaging states
  2. Melt butter in sauce pan
  3. Add your heavy cream and let it sit until it gets thicker but don't let it boil
  4. Add parmsean cheese and stir it until the cheese has melted
  5. Start to add your salt and pepper
  6. In another pan, melt more butter
  7. Add the shrimp to that second pan and cook until pink and tender (flip the shrimp to make sure both sides are cooked evenly)
  8. Add your garlic to the pan and cook for a few more seconds
  9. By this time your pasta should be cooked...So, drain the pasta then add your alfredo sauce
  10. Start to ad your other seasonings - Paprika, Cayenne pepper, and any other seasoning of your choice
  11. Place your pasta in a bowl and add your shrimp on top
  12. Sprinkle a little parsley on top and ENJOY!!
